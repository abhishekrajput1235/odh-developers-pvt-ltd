WHO WE ARE

ODH Developers Private Limited is a company incorporated, a well-established, professionally run company which is engaged in the business of Infrastructure Development and Real Estate Projects.

As one of the fastest growing and most trusted real estate development brands in Uttar Pradesh, India, the group is engaged in developing housing projects in Kanpur, Lucknow, Yamuna Expressway and Greater Noida. It has a diverse portfolio, with interests in construction and Contracting.

The company has young, dynamic and enterprising experts who have the passion to develop and deliver quality projects. Ethical propriety and transparency are the bedrock of their corporate philosophy ODH Developers is has been promoted by people with solid experience in the real estate industry.

ODH Developers owes its meteoric rise to highest echelons of the real estate industry to the ceaseless efforts we've been making since our inception to understand the unspoken needs of customers and go above and beyond their expectations.

WHAT WE DO

ODH Developers is a multi- disciplinary real estate company with proven experience and competence in offering property and investment advisory services to clients in Uttar Pradesh. We offer residential & commercial property and investment advisory services to clients, ensuring that we give value for the confidence reposed in us at all times.
