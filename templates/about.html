Who We Are

A Legacy of Trust and Excellence in Real Estate

ODH Developers Private Limited is a well-established, professionally run company engaged in Infrastructure Development and Real Estate Projects, and is one of the fastest growing and most trusted real estate brands in Uttar Pradesh, India. The group is developing housing projects in Kanpur, Lucknow, Yamuna Expressway and Greater Noida, with a diverse portfolio spanning construction and contracting. The company has young, dynamic and enterprising experts who have the passion to develop and deliver quality projects. Ethical propriety and transparency are the bedrock of our corporate philosophy. ODH Developers has been promoted by leaders with solid experience in the real estate industry. Our meteoric rise in the real estate industry is owed to our ceaseless efforts to understand the unspoken needs of our customers and to go above and beyond their expectations. This commitment enables us to develop world-class properties and offer them at the most competitive prices in the industry.

Our credo, Credibility of Commitments, reflects our mission to offer superior construction quality – from the design-driven facades crafted by our in-house architects, to flawless interiors tailored to each market, supported by world-class service. ODH Developers is where luxury meets economy. We are committed to bringing different facets of quality living and lifestyle amenities through our unique projects. Our vision reflects a strong commitment to society, bringing you closer to your desires and aligning perfectly with your dreams.

Discover More About Us

At ODH Developers, we are engaged in delivering exceptional real estate solutions that blend innovation with reliability. Our expertise spans across residential communities, commercial spaces, and investment advisory services, all designed to meet diverse client needs. We focus on creating projects that reflect quality construction, modern design, and long-term value. With a strong commitment to transparency and customer trust, we ensure that every property we develop is more than just a structure — it is a step towards a better lifestyle and future.

At ODH Developers, our mission is to build more than just properties — we build experiences. Guided by our credo of Credibility of Commitments, we strive to deliver developments that combine architectural excellence, sustainable practices, and unmatched quality, creating lasting value for our customers and communities.

Our vision has been the same since the beginning, to be a dynamic and innovative real estate company with a passionate commitment to provide superbly designed and built homes in excellent locations. We focus on developing township and building apartments adhering to our trademark high standards and catering to all budgets. After successfully delivering excellent homes to hundreds of happy families, we aim to continue building homes and in the process, creating communities.

We are professional and adaptable to the varied needs of our clients. We leverage our collective strength to provide exceptional real estate solutions across residential, commercial, and investment domains. Our versatility lies in our ability to understand unique requirements and deliver projects that seamlessly combine quality, innovation, and functionality. Whether it’s designing modern homes, developing large-scale townships, or guiding clients in property investments, we bring the same commitment to excellence in every endeavor. This adaptability ensures that ODH Developers remains a trusted partner, capable of shaping spaces that cater to diverse lifestyles while exceeding expectations.
